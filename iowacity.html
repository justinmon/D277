IOWA CITY

Welcome to Iowa City, Home of the Hawkeyes!

Incorporated in the year 1839, Iowa City is located in east-central Iowa, 27 miles south of Cedar Rapids. Iowa City is classified as a urban city. The city was originally founded as the state capital. Iowa City lost the state capital title to Des Moines in the year 1857. (7)

The economy is largely based around the University of Iowa, and is the main centerpiece of the city. (7) Iowa City truly is a unique city that is bustling with energy, academia, and rich dining!
