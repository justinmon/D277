CEDAR RAPIDS

Welcome to Cedar Rapids, the City of Five Seasons!

Incorporated in the year 1849, Cedar Rapids is the second-largest city in Iowa. The city lies on the Cedar River in eastern Iowa. Cedar Rapids is classified as a suburban city. The city was originally named Rapids City because of the strong and abundant waterpower from the Cedar River. (2)

Cedar Rapids offers a unique blend of urban amenities and fast growing family friendly suburban neighborhoods. Cedar Rapids is a welcoming city for anybody!
